In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)

Our IVF lab is equipped with state-of-the-art embryology systems and internationally trained specialists.

We provide individualized IVF plans, embryo freezing, and embryo transfer techniques to maximize your success rate while supporting your emotional well-being throughout the process.

Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)

A gentle, low-intervention procedure to help couples with mild fertility challenges.

IUI can be ideal for couples with unexplained infertility or mild male factor infertility. It involves placing washed sperm directly into the uterus during ovulation to increase chances of conception.

Egg Freezing & Fertility Preservation

Empowering women to plan their family on their own timeline.

We offer advanced egg freezing and preservation techniques for women wishing to delay pregnancy for personal, medical, or professional reasons — with safety, compassion, and long-term care in mind.

Male Fertility & Andrology

Male infertility contributes to nearly half of all fertility challenges.

Our male fertility unit offers semen analysis, hormonal testing, and treatment for conditions such as low sperm count or motility — with discretion and expert care.

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that fertility treatment raises many questions. Here are some of the most common ones we receive.

How do I know if I need fertility treatment?

If you have been trying to conceive for over a year (or 6 months if over age 35), we recommend a fertility assessment for both partners.

What is the success rate of IVF?

Success rates vary based on age, diagnosis, and treatment type. Our clinic achieves pregnancy rates above the national average in most age groups.

Can male infertility be treated?

Yes. Many cases of male infertility are treatable through medications, lifestyle changes, or procedures like ICSI and sperm retrieval.

Is fertility treatment emotionally difficult?

It can be, which is why we offer counseling, support groups, and a caring team to guide and support you every step of the way.

Can I freeze my eggs for the future?

Absolutely. Egg freezing is a safe and effective way to preserve fertility for women who wish to delay pregnancy or face medical treatments.
